I'm Ranking All the Burgers I Eat in London

Published on 06 January 2018
food

Recently I've been sampling some of the burgers London has to offer. I decided it might be nice to rank them all in order of my personal preference. I'll keep the list updated as I eat more burgers. For now I've just hit the chains:

The Mark Harmon Burgers in London League Table

Rank Burger Place Location Description Price
1 Honest Burgers Hammersmith This burger was delicious, pink in the middle and very tasty. The rosemary fries are considered by Honest to be mandatory for the experience. I disagree, this burger doesn't need the fries. £9.75
2 Shake Shack Tottenham Court Road My first impression was that this burger was a little small, still served pink and tasty though! £5.50
3 Five Guys Westfield Shepherds Bush A very nice burger, big and juicy. I think my only issue is that it's a little overcooked for my taste and the bun could be firmer, it seemed a little floppy. £8.00
4 Byron Hammersmith I had heard good things about this burger going in so had high expectations. I was actually a little dissapointed. It's a decent burger but was a little dry. £10.00
